About Us

We work at the formulation level — not just the trading level. Factory-integrated, specification-driven, and built for manufacturers who demand more than a price list.

Who We Are

Precision Chemistry.
Global Reach.

Balance Industry Group is a factory-integrated chemical supplier established in 2016 and headquartered in Zhengzhou, China. We operate with independent export credentials and maintain direct, long-term partnerships with our production facilities — giving us influence at the formulation and specification level that a conventional trading company simply cannot offer.

Our focus spans two critical segments of global manufacturing: PVC resins and plasticizers for the plastics and polymer industries, and Acetylene Carbon Black for advanced conductive applications including lithium-ion batteries and high-voltage cable systems.

Because we are embedded in the production process — not standing outside it — we can align grades, adjust specifications, and respond to custom requirements with the speed and authority of a manufacturer's representative, backed by the flexibility of an independent supplier.

What We Supply

Two Core Product Pillars

Our portfolio is structured around the complementary needs of plastics processors and advanced materials manufacturers.

Base Resins & Additives
PVC Resin, PVC Paste Resin & Plasticizers
Suspension PVC  ·  Emulsion / Micro-Suspension Paste  ·  DOP / DOTP / DINP

Serving manufacturers in pipes & fittings, cable & wire, synthetic leather, flooring, vinyl gloves, and flexible packaging. Consistent K-value, viscosity stability, and full COA on every shipment.

Conductive Materials
Acetylene Carbon Black
Ultra-High Purity  ·  Conductive Grade  ·  Battery Grade

Manufactured through controlled exothermic decomposition of high-purity acetylene gas. Engineered for lithium-ion batteries, dry cells, lead-acid batteries, high-voltage cable shielding, and EMI-shielding conductive polymers.
