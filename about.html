Company Profile

Balance Industry was established in the year 2001, who is a professional and comprehensive chemical group in China specialized in Chlor-Alkali chemicals and Petrochemicals based on the advantages of abundant resources. Meantime, as for meeting more customers’ requirements in the domestic and international market, we always pay more attention to the cooperation with other strong and powerful companies to enable us to provide better products and service for the honorable customers in the diversified industries.

In Balance Industry, we see customers as business partners. We are committed to their success. By our definition, an appreciative customer is the mark of a successful company. In return for investing in us, our customers get to share our wealth of experience and innovative thinking as well as our guarantee of regular and reliable supply of high-quality products.

Since our incorporation, we are fully committed to serve and deliver high quality and competitive products to end users. Creating a win-win relationship with customers will always remain our uncompromised priority in order to sustain long term business friendship. We believe our customers’ preference for us, as well as trust and cooperation form a solid basis for success.
