PVC Resin

K-58  ·  K-67  ·  K-70  ·  K-71

Suspension-polymerized PVC resin produced by leading ISO-certified Chinese manufacturers. Each grade is characterized by its K-value — a measure of average molecular weight — determining processability, mechanical properties, and end-use suitability.

We supply consistent, batch-verified resin with full Certificate of Analysis (COA), MSDS, and SGS inspection on request. Available in 25 kg bags or 1,000 kg jumbo bags; minimum order from 1 FCL (20–25 MT).

PVC Paste Resin

P-440  ·  P-450  ·  P-70  ·  Custom Grades

Plasticizers

DOP  ·  DOTP  ·  DINP

We supply the three primary plasticizers used in PVC compounding: DOP (general purpose), DOTP (phthalate-free, REACH-compliant), and DINP (high molecular weight, low volatility). Each product is sourced from established Chinese producers with consistent purity specifications.

Available in 200 L drums or ISO tank containers. Purity typically ≥99.5%. Full COA, MSDS, and packing list provided. Suitable for flexible PVC film and plastisol formulations.

Conductive Grade  ·  Battery Grade

Acetylene Carbon Black

Ultra-High Purity  ·  Controlled Exothermic Decomposition Process

Manufactured by controlled exothermic decomposition of high-purity acetylene gas, our carbon black delivers exceptional electrical conductivity, ultra-low ash content, and a characteristic 3D chain-like structure that maximises liquid absorption — critical for lithium battery electrolyte retention.

Available in standard powder form and compressed pellet form. Packaging: 10 kg or 20 kg kraft bags inside cartons; palletized for container loading. Full lot traceability, COA, and MSDS provided. Sample quantities available for qualification testing.
